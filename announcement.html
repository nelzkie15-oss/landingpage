OBITUARY – Capt. Francisco D. Menor



We regret to announce that Capt. Francisco D. Menor, former President of Magsaysay MOL Marine, Inc (MMMI) passed away last March 18, 2020 at the Medical City (Ortigas City). His body lies at Arlington Memorial Chapel, No. 12 Gregorio Araneta Ave., Quezon City. The internment is on March 26, 2020(Morning) in Loyola, Marikina City. Let us offer our deepest and heartfelt condolence to the Menor Family.
